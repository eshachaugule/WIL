Nita Ambani

There are a lot of people who has written about Nita Ambani, who is the wife of Mukesh Ambani, the richest man in India. Now the media has also started writing about Shloka Mehta, the daughter in law of Nita ambani and most of us are not at all aware about the fact that how Nita Ambani was chosen to be wife of Mukesh Ambani. There are a lot of factors about Nita Ambani that we all might not be aware about. As we all know, Nita Ambani is the chairman of Reliance Industries' philanthropic arm Reliance Foundation, which focuses on running philanthropic activities for underprivileged throughout the year.

Here we will go through some unknown facts about Nita Ambani who is one of the richest women in India and we can also get a glimpse of her journey from a middle class Bharatnatyam dancer from Gujarat to becoming one of the most powerful women in India. Nita Ambani hails from a middle class Gujarati family and her name by birth was Nita Dalal.

Nita Ambani's father Ravindrabhai Dalal was a senior executive of the Birla group. Nita Ambani was a graduate in Commerce from Narsee Monjee College of Commerce and Economics, and she started teaching until her luck brought her in touch with Mukesh Ambani.

Nita Ambani is a trained Bharatnatyam dancer and her amazing dancing performances often took her around India. During one of her performances, Mukesh Ambani's late father Dhirubhai Ambani started liking her and decided to make Nita Amabani the wife of his son Mukesh Ambani. Nita Ambani accepted the proposal, but it was on a condition that she will be allowed to continue her profession as a teacher.

Nita Ambani said that for almost 17 years, she preferred to choose away even after being Mukesh's wife. But now she is the main spearhead in driving the Dhirubhai Ambani International School since 2013 and the school has become one of the most popular educational institutions in Mumbai.
