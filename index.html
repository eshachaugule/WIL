WOMEN LEADERS

Women's empowerment is the process in which women elaborate and recreate what it is in a circumstance that they previously were denied. Empowerment can be defined in many ways, however, when talking about women's empowerment, empowerment means accepting and allowing people (women) who are on the outside of the decision-making process into it.

Throughout history, there have been many women leaders who have made significant contribution to the field. Here, we will see, learn and get inspired by a few of them.
