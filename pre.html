Priyanka Chopra

Priyanka Chopra was born on 18 July 1982, and her hometown is in small-town Bareilly. She gained name and fame not only in India but she has also proved herself at the international level. She is a celebrity who has inspired many girls to achieve their dreams. Here are the few glimpses of Desi girl's life which are both interesting as well as inspiring.

At the age of 13, Priyanka Chopra moved to the united states to live with her aunt. She faced racism and being bullied by her African and American batchmates

She said in an interview that "I had white marks on her legs but I worked hard and today my leg sell 12 brands".

She returned from the US and joined modelling to build some confidence. She won the title of Miss World in the year 2000 at the age of 18

Bollywood gave her ways after becoming the miss- world and she made her debut in Film The Hero: Love story of Spy in 2003.

She has done many big movies and established herself as a big actress in Bollywood. She also won national award for her excellent performance in film 'Fashion'.

After becoming successful in Bollywood she made her entry in Hollywood with American TV show Quantico. She also played the important role in Hollywood movie Baywatch She also amazed people with her singing talent by releasing her album' In my city'.

She got married to Singer, actor and producer, Nick Jonas, who is 1/3rd of the band 'Jonas Brothers'. They had a 4 day wedding ceremony in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, India and got married on 1st December 2018 in western style and 2nd December in Punjabi style. We are hardcore Priyanka Chopra Fans.
