About

We have created this website to increase awarness about our past legendary women who created a legacy for themselves and for the future generation of women. The path that they created for us yesteryear, is a guiding light through the darkness, and has helped us to realise and achieve our dreams.

We also want to make young girls aware of the achievements of our current women leaders who make us realise that that anything is possible in today's world, and that no one can tell us that we can't do something.

We would appreciate your support in helping us grow our platform and thus helping us build a world where the future generations feel safe, secure and happy to follow their dreams.
